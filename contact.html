FAQ

Is there an age restriction for entry?

Yes, you must be at least 21 years old to enter the club.

Do you have parking for cars?

Yes, we have our own parking lot for guests. Convenient parking is available near the club.

Is photography or videography allowed in the club?

Generally, photography and videography are not permitted in the club, but there are exceptions for special events. Details can be obtained from the club's administration.

Do you offer VIP table service or private rooms?

Yes, we have luxurious VIP areas and private cabins for our guests. To make a reservation, please contact us by phone or email

Do you have a loyalty program for regular customers?

Yes, we have a loyalty program that allows our regular customers to receive discounts and other benefits. Details can be obtained from our staff.

Does your club organize themed parties or special events?

Yes, we often host themed parties, special events, and live performances. Visit our website or social media pages to find out about upcoming events and activities.

Do you serve food?

We offer a selection of light bites and snacks, but we're primarily a nightclub.
